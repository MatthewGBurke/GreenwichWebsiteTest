Welcome to Greenwich Sports Club

A community sports club in Sydney's Lower North Shore with teams in Senior Mens and Womens Football, MiniRoos, Junior Mixed and Girl's Football, NetSetGo and Junior Girls Netball.

Sports

Whether you are new to the area or just looking for a friendly place to play sport, GSC is a fantastic choice. If you are interested in playing football for Greenwich Sports Club, please contact the appropriate age group coordinator with any enquiries. For netball, please contact the netball convenor.
