Active Kids

The Active Kids programme is a NSW Government initiative to encourage school age children to participate in physical activity by providing a means tested $50 voucher to offset the cost of membership or participation fees.

The programme commenced on 31 Jan 2018 and the NSW Government information is here for parents/guardians.

Football

The FFA player registration system provides parents with the ability to enter an Active Kids voucher code before proceeding to the payment step.

Netball

Netball NSW also accepts Active Kids vouchers for Netball registrations.

Please note that the voucher can't be split between sports.
